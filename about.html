About Us

At International Cyber Crime Investigation Bureau (ICCIB), we are a specialized Fraud Investigation and Scam Intelligence Team dedicated to detecting, tracking, exposing, and helping stop scammers worldwide.

Our team consists of experienced fraud analysts, cyber investigators, financial intelligence specialists, and digital forensic experts who work together to uncover hidden scam operations, trace stolen funds, and identify the individuals behind fraudulent schemes.

We operate with integrity, accuracy, confidentiality, and professionalism, ensuring that every investigation meets international investigative and legal standards.

Our Mission

To protect people, recover truth, and help bring scammers to justice.

Executive Leadership

STOPPING CYBER CRIME

Cyber Crime Investigation Bureau

At the International Cyber Crime Investigation Bureau (ICCIB), our mission is to actively detect, investigate, and prevent cybercriminal activities worldwide. From online fraud to identity theft, we work to stop digital threats before they harm individuals or businesses.

PROTECTING THE DIGITAL WORLD

DIGITAL WORLD

ICCIB is committed to safeguarding the online space for everyone. Through advanced digital forensics, cyber intelligence, and global collaboration, we help ensure a safer internet where personal data, financial assets, and digital networks are protected from cyber threats.
